## Standing laws for all marketing work

These repeat the load-bearing rules so a fresh session cannot miss them. Sources: root `CLAUDE.md`, this repo's `CLAUDE.md`, `raceproof-docs/brand/`.

1. **Read before writing.** `raceproof-docs/CANONICAL_STATE.md` before any claim about the product; the relevant brand/voice doc before any copy.
2. **Positioning law.** Raceproof is not anti-cheat. Never "validation" or "verification." Trust is personalization.
3. **IP law.** What and why, never how. No weights, constants, baselines, or calibration parameters. The r = 0.62 finding and the PT-T trajectory values are founder-corpus data findings cleared for editorial use; algorithm mechanism questions are off-limits even internally.
4. **Factual claims validated before publication.** The standard: could this sentence survive a public challenge from the company named in it?
5. **Single backlog.** Work items live on `raceproof-docs/TASK_BOARD.md`. This repo holds state and material, never a backlog.
6. **Writing rules** (all content): no em dashes, ever. Specific numbers. Longer fluid sentences as the primary vehicle, short sentences for reinforcement. "And the effect..." bridges data to meaning. No generic CTAs. "Raceproof" lowercase p in prose ("RaceProof" only for RaceProof LLC). "Fingerprint" and "Effort Signature" are banned. A seat never "sits/sat/rests/perches"; a seat is a value, a threshold falls at or lives at a duration.
7. **Emails** are first person from Holden, no "we," links as underlined text not buttons, sign-off "Holden" with no title.
8. **Output routing.** Marketing and editorial output files and session summaries are committed to this repo (`raceproof-marketing`), never to `raceproof-docs`. Torque article HTML lives in `raceproof-torque/articles/`.
