## Key decisions

- **Video over static, as a reach test:** low engagement diagnosed as structural (carousels reach followers only; Reels reach non-followers). Format is the variable being tested, with brand-voice vs founder-voice as the secondary experiment (V1 vs V4 on different accounts).
- **Four script arcs drafted, two shipped.** V2 "Two Riders" rejected for now: requires an invented second athlete, conflicts with the verified-data-only standard. V3 "Moving Number" parked as the natural second-cycle video once a larger offset move exists in the corpus.
- **Title methodology established** (also in `channels/VIDEO.md`): riff on common spoken language, swap the unit; state or pose a resolvable question, never tease; avoid ambiguous words ("long" = distance or time; "minutes" is unambiguous); the title should be the social hook verbatim.

## Learnings / process corrections

- **Pre-publish checklist gains a hard item: no article ships with an og:image TODO.** Run `build_og_cards.py` at publish time and after any retitle (the card bakes the title in).
- **Retitle mechanics are now a known recipe** (see What shipped #1). LinkedIn caches OG scrapes; a never-shared new slug scrapes clean, but any previously shared URL needs Post Inspector purge.
- **`build_og_cards.py` regenerates every card;** when only one is needed, revert the others before committing.
- **Same-position text crossfades double-expose** in video; stagger exits and entries. Reels UI eats the bottom ~370px; keep content above y≈1550.
- **Lime discipline holds in motion:** measured values and URL only. Founder-voice video carries no brand chrome until the end card.
